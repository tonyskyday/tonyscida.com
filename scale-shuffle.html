Features

Random Generator

Tap to get a random scale from 15+ types across all 17 keys. No more defaulting to the same comfortable patterns.

Sheet Music

See every scale written out in standard notation. Great for sight-reading practice and learning new patterns.

Progress Tracking

Track your practice streaks and stats. Build consistency with daily reminders and see how far you’ve come.

Customizable

Choose which scale types and keys to include. Focus on what you need to work on, skip what you don’t.

Privacy Policy

Overview

Scale Shuffle is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Scale Shuffle collects no personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or any third-party data collection tools.

Local Storage

Your practice data, preferences, and statistics are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Tips & Purchases

Scale Shuffle may offer optional tip jar purchases through Apple’s StoreKit framework. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not receive any personal financial information from these transactions.

Children’s Privacy

Scale Shuffle does not collect data from any user, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page with a revised date below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at tony@tonyscida.com.
